FAQ Question
How do I use Soribaro service?
FAQ Answer
Here's how to use Soribaro service: 1. Sign Up - Visit the Soribaro website and complete the sign-up process. - Once email verification is complete, you can use the service. 2. Upload Files - After logging in, click the 'Upload File' button. - Upload audio or video files. - Supported formats: MP3, WAV, MP4, AVI, etc. 3. Select Options - Choose the type of work (transcription, translation, subtitles, etc.). - Enter the number of speakers and any special requirements. - Set the desired completion date. 4. Payment - Review the quote and proceed with payment. - Various payment methods are supported (card, bank transfer, points). 5. Work Progress - Once work begins, you can check the progress in real-time. - Monitor work status from My Page. 6. Completion and Download - You will receive a notification when work is completed. - Download and review the completed files. For additional inquiries, please contact our customer service (1588-1234).
